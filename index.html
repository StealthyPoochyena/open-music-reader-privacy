Open Music Reader — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-18

Open Music Reader is a free, offline music-reading app. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Your content — PDF scores, annotations, jump markers, concerts and setlists, and app settings — is stored only on your device. It is never uploaded to us or anyone else.

Camera: if you scan sheet music, the camera is used only to capture pages, which are converted to a PDF stored on your device. Document detection runs on-device (Google ML Kit); images are not sent to any server by this app.

No accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party data sharing.

Backups you export are plain files you control; restoring reads only a file you choose.

Because no data leaves your device, there is nothing for us to access, retain, or delete.
